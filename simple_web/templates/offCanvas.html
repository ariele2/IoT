Welcome Big Brother!

With me you can control the smart building system :)

I have 4 different tabs - home page, status, sensors and scheduler

home page - Turn the system on/off, watch how much time is left until a schedule starts/ends and generate csv files of system data from the past.

status - Watch the status of the sensors - you can know if a sensor is not working and why.

sensors - Here you can see all of the system's sensors details - picture, location and description.

scheduler - Our system is pretty versatile, you can pick a date range in which the system will work.
